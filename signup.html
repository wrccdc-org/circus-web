Registration Information

Teams are registered by a faculty or staff advisor from the participating institution.

Who Should Register?

  • Undergraduate student teams with a designated faculty or staff advisor.
  • Institutions wishing to field multiple teams across Division I and Division II.

Registration Steps

  1. Review the 2026 CIRCUS rules in full.
  2. Confirm your division (I or II) based on institutional type.
  3. Identify a faculty or staff advisor to act as team representative.
  4. Gather team information (institution, division, team name, roster, advisor contact info, etc.).
  5. Submit the online registration form during the registration window.
